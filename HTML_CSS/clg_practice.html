Class Test 2 Suggestion Answers for Human Values and Ethics.


1. Nyaya: Nyaya means justice or righteousness. It embodies the idea of fair and ethical conduct, emphasizing the importance of moral principles in human actions.
2. Spirality in Spirituality: Spirality in spirituality refers to the evolutionary journey of the spirit. It underscores continuous growth, learning, and self-discovery on the spiritual path.
3. Important Values of Human Relationships: Trust, respect, and communication are pivotal values in human relationships. They form the foundation for understanding, empathy, and lasting connections.
4. Concept of Karma: Karma is the principle of cause and effect, emphasizing that one's actions shape their destiny. It promotes responsibility and accountability for one's deeds.
5. Ideas of Rabindranath Tagore: Tagore emphasized values like education for holistic development, cultural appreciation, and a harmonious blend of traditional and modern values.
